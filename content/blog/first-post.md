I started this project feeling completely overwhelmed. Hugo looked fast and clean, but the folder structure and templates were confusing at first.

What helped most was breaking it into tiny steps:

1. Make one page render correctly.
2. Reuse a shared layout.
3. Add navigation links.
4. Improve styling after the structure works.

That approach turned a vague task into a series of clear wins. If you're stuck, do the smallest possible next step and keep moving.
